Our Identity

Culture, Values & the People Behind Bingju

We build technology — but first we build people, principles, and a culture that makes excellence sustainable.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Purpose

To empower businesses and individuals worldwide through innovative digital technology, world-class software, and seamless mobile experiences — making complex technology accessible, reliable, and impactful for every user, regardless of geography.
To become a globally recognized leader in digital technology solutions — a company known for the quality of its products, the integrity of its team, and its positive impact on communities and businesses across every continent.
We exist to bridge the gap between great ideas and powerful technology. Every app we ship, every platform we build, and every brand we help shape is a step toward a more connected and empowered world.
We take legal and ethical responsibility seriously. All our products adhere to GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and applicable regional data protection laws. Our apps follow Google Play Policies and Apple App Store Guidelines at every stage of development and publishing.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

Innovation First
We constantly push the boundaries of what technology can achieve. Every product we build begins with the question: how can we make this better than anything currently available?
Uncompromising Quality
Quality is non-negotiable. From the first line of code to the final pixel, we demand excellence in everything we create and every service we deliver.
Global Perspective
We think globally from day one. Our products and services are designed for diverse cultures, languages, and markets — built to succeed on every continent.
People-Centric
Our team members are our greatest asset. We invest in continuous learning, personal growth, mental wellness, and creating an environment where talent flourishes.
Trust & Integrity
Honesty and transparency underpin every client relationship and internal interaction. We keep our promises, admit our mistakes, and always act with integrity.
Continuous Growth
The digital landscape never stands still, and neither do we. We embrace change, learn from every project, and continuously evolve our skills and practices.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

2017 — Founding
Suzhou Bingju Network Technology Co., Ltd Established
Founded in Suzhou's Wuzhong District Economic Development Zone, Bingju Technology began with a core focus on web technology development and digital services for businesses.
2018 — First Major Projects
Enterprise Platform Delivery & Software Division Launch
Successfully delivered our first enterprise-scale web platforms and officially launched the software development division to serve a growing demand for custom business applications.
2019 — Mobile Expansion
Mobile App Development Division Established
We entered the mobile development space, beginning our journey of publishing consumer applications on Google Play and the Apple App Store — quickly growing to an initial portfolio of 10+ apps.
2020 — Live Streaming & Content
Internet Live Streaming Platform Services Launched
Recognizing the explosive growth of live content, Bingju Technology launched dedicated live streaming infrastructure services and began integrating real-time content features into our mobile app ecosystem.
2021 — Global E-Commerce
E-Commerce Retail Operations & Brand Consulting Division
We expanded into cross-border e-commerce retail — managing digital storefronts for electronics, daily goods, and merchandise — and formally launched our brand consulting and strategy practice.
2022–2023 — Scale & Compliance
50+ Apps Published, GDPR & CCPA Compliance Framework
Our mobile portfolio surpassed 50 published applications. We implemented a comprehensive global compliance framework covering GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and major app store policy requirements.
2024–2025 — Advanced Monetization
Full Ad Monetization Suite & Management App Ecosystem
Integrated advanced multi-platform ad monetization across our app portfolio (AdMob, Meta, Unity, IronSource, AppLovin, and more) and launched a proprietary mobile management dashboard for enterprise clients.
2026 — Today
Global Reach & Continued Innovation
Serving clients across Europe, North America, and global markets from our Suzhou headquarters — with a growing team, an expanding app portfolio, and a relentless commitment to digital excellence.

Responsibility

Corporate Social Responsibility

Digital Accessibility

All our apps and platforms are designed with accessibility in mind — meeting WCAG 2.1 AA standards to ensure equal access for users with disabilities worldwide.

Child Safety & COPPA Compliance

We strictly enforce age verification and child protection policies across all our applications. Apps directed at users under 13 fully comply with COPPA and equivalent international regulations.

Data Privacy Leadership

We are committed to transparent data practices. Users always know what data is collected, why it is collected, and how to exercise their rights under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws.

Environmental Responsibility

We optimize our digital infrastructure for energy efficiency, choose cloud providers with green energy commitments, and encourage sustainable work practices within our organization.
